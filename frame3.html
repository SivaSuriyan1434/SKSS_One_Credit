Fourth meeting of the G20 Sustainable Finance Working Group (SFWG)

17 September 2021

  On Friday 17 September 2021, the G20 Sustainable Finance Working Group members met virtually to discuss new versions of the 2021 Sustainable Finance Synthesis Report and of the G20 Sustainable Finance Roadmap, which include comments previously raised by G20 members.
  There is broad agreement on the actions and recommendations proposed in the two documents which will be finalized in the coming days. Regarding the Roadmap, the group concurred that the document is well-focused and sufficiently flexible to be expanded, in the next few years, to explore the challenges and opportunities related to other sustainability issues beyond those related to climate change. First G20 Conference on Women Empowerment

27 August 2021

  The G20 Conference on Women’s Empowerment took place on 26 August 2021 in Santa Margherita Ligure. This is the first time that a dedicated event on women’s empowerment has been planned in the G20 system, focusing on the quality of women’s work, the implementation of policies aimed at increasing women’s participation in positions of leadership, the protection of women’s rights, and on tackling gender-based violence.
  The Conference was opened with a message by the Italian Prime Minister, Mario Draghi and a speech by the Italian Minister for Equal Opportunities and Family, Elena Bonetti, followed by remarks from the President of the Senate, Maria Elisabetta Casellati, and interventions by the Vice-President of the Chamber of Deputies, Maria Edera Spadoni and the EU Commissioner for Equality, Helena Dalli.
